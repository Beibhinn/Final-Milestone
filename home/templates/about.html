About us

Who we are

We are a relatively new startup, focused on trying to create a better user experience for lovers of the internet

What we do

We fix whatever bugs you have come across that are impacting your user experience. We also welcome suggestions from our clients regarding new features they'd like to see implemented. After all, when it comes to user experience, who better than the users themselves to let us know how things could be improved!

How it works

You can use the dashboard to browse known bugs or previously suggested features. If you see a bug you've come across that you'd also like fixed, simply upvote by clicking the upvote icon: . If you see a feature you'd also like to have, you can make a donation to support that feature. If you've come across a new bug, or have a brand new feature in mind, please feel free to add it. (Please be sure to search properly first, to avoid duplicate issues and so we can prioritise our efforts accordingly). Other users will be able to see and support your bug or feature by upvoting or donating to it. Registered users can also post comments on issues. Please note, that guests will only be able to browse issues and read comments. You must register to upvote, donate or post a comment. Bugs/ features you've added, as well as ones you've upvoted or donated to will be visible in your account.

Our pledge to you

Above all else, we value transparency and honesty, so we like to lay it all out for our users.

  • All bugs will be fixed eventually, however we do prioritise the bugs with the most upvotes, so be sure to cast your vote.
  • Features will only be developed and implemented if there is enough interest from our users. As features are donation-based, we promise to always spend at least 50% of our time working on the feature with the most donations.
  • We will keep our users up with the status of bugs and features via our dashboard, where they can easily see if the issue hasn't yet been started, if it is in progress, in review, or complete.

So that's it! Sparkle on, you beautiful unicorns
